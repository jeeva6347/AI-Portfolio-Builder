## 1. Getting Started & Signing Up
1. Visit the home page and click **Get Started Free** or **Sign Up**.
2. Register an account using your email or choose **Continue with Google** / **Continue with GitHub**.
3. Choose a subscription tier from the **Billing** tab:
   - **Free Plan**: 1 Portfolio, 3 AI Resume imports.
   - **Premium Plan**: Unlimited Portfolios, custom domain connections, premium templates.

---

## 2. AI Resume Import
To fast-track building your portfolio:
1. Navigate to the **Resume Import** page.
2. Drag and drop your `PDF` or `Word` resume file.
3. Review the extracted JSON results on the review panel.
4. Select **Overwrite** (replace existing content) or **Merge** (append fields).
5. Click **Generate Portfolio** to instantly pre-populate your details.

---

## 3. Visual Builder Workspace
The Portfolio Builder workspace provides a live, side-by-side editing experience:
1. **Left Panel**: Click through tabs to edit personal details, education, certificates, work experiences, projects, and testimonials.
2. **Right Panel**: Observe live, non-blink iframe preview updates. Switch device categories (Desktop, Laptop, Tablet, Mobile) or adjust zoom multipliers.
3. **Select Theme**: Click **Select Theme** tab, browse available designs, and click **Activate Theme**.
4. **Auto-Save**: The editor saves your work in the background with status saving flags.

---

## 4. Publishing Your Portfolio
Once you're satisfied with your portfolio:
1. Navigate to the **GitHub Publish** page.
2. Connect your GitHub Profile via secure OAuth.
3. Select an existing repository or create a new public/private repository.
4. Click **Publish to GitHub Pages**. The compiler packaging converter will build static assets and deploy your website automatically.

---

## 5. Setting Up Custom Domains
For Premium subscribers wishing to connect custom domains:
1. Navigate to **Custom Domains** on your portfolio settings.
2. Add your custom domain (e.g., `www.yourname.com`).
3. Add the required `CNAME` or `TXT` DNS records in your domain provider control panel (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare) according to instructions.
4. Click **Verify DNS**. The platform will issue an SSL certificate and route requests automatically.

---

## 6. Analyzing Visitor Traffic
Track views and performance stats:
1. Click **Analytics** on the sidebar.
2. View visitor counts over the past 30 days via Chart.js line charts.
3. Inspect breakdowns of visitor device categories, referrers URLs, and countries.
4. Click **SEO Editor** to update robots.txt rules, search keywords, and preview social metadata cards.
5. Click **Speed Diagnostics** to view layout weights and performance suggestions.

---

## 7. Collaborating with Teams
Work with coworkers, clients, or design partners:
1. Go to **Team Workspace** page.
2. Click **Create Organization Workspace** and give it a name.
3. From the members tab, send email invitations specifying roles:
   - **Owner**: Full access, transfer ownership, billing manager.
   - **Admin**: Manage members, link/unlink portfolios.
   - **Editor**: Edit portfolio contents and themes.
   - **Viewer**: Read-only previews.
4. Copy the unique invitation links or invite via email to collaborate.
